3.2.2 The ENDATA Indicator Card

The ENDATA indicator card simply announces the end of the input data. The data for a particular problem, in the form of indicator and data cards, must lie between a NAME and an ENDATA card. The syntax for the ENDATA card is given in Figure 3.4.
